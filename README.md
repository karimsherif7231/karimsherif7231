# 🧑‍💻 Karim Sherif
<img src="https://readme-typing-svg.herokuapp.com?font=Fira+Code&pause=1000&color=F7F7F7&width=435&lines=MERN+Stack+Developer" alt="Typing SVG" />
I’m Karim Sherif, a Front-End developer with three years of self-driven learning and ongoing practical development experience. I work with the MERN stack, including React, Next.js, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB, in addition to familiarity with PostgreSQL, Laravel (PHP), and a basic understanding of Nest.js.

My interest lies in building reliable and user-focused web applications while continuously improving my skills and staying aligned with modern development practices. I approach problem-solving with patience and curiosity, aiming to create solutions that are practical, maintainable, and genuinely helpful to users.

I’m also exploring AI automation using n8n to enhance workflow efficiency and support smarter product development. Alongside web technologies, I remain interested in the role of digital tools in education and how they can simplify learning experiences for students and educators.

I value teamwork, clear communication, adaptability, and responsible leadership, and I’m always open to learning from others and contributing wherever I can. My long-term goal is to build a product-focused business that delivers meaningful and positive impact through technology.
